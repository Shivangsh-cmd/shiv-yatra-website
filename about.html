About Us

Your trusted partners in authentic Bengali travel experiences

Our Story

Welcome to Shiv Yatra Homestay, where authentic Bengali hospitality meets unforgettable travel experiences. Founded by Suman Chandra and Dipannita Chandra Sarkar, we specialize in creating personalized journeys that showcase the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of Bengal and the eastern Himalayas. Our passion for travel and deep local knowledge ensures that every guest discovers hidden gems and creates lasting memories with us.

Meet Our Founders

Suman Chandra

With years of experience in hospitality and deep knowledge of Bengal's cultural heritage, Suman ensures every guest feels at home while discovering the region's hidden treasures.

Dipannita Chandra Sarkar

Passionate about authentic cuisine and local traditions, Dipannita brings the warmth of Bengali hospitality to every meal and cultural experience we offer.
